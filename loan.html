Loan Prediction using Machine Learning Algorithms

A Housing Finance Company, a prominent player in the home loan industry with a nationwide presence across urban, semi-urban, and rural areas, aims to streamline its loan eligibility process.Currently, customers apply for home loans, and the company manually evaluates their eligibility based on various factors such as gender, marital status, education, income, and credit history. The company intends to automate this process using machine learning algorithms to enhance efficiency and provide real-time decision-making.

Problem Statement

The primary challenge is to develop a robust machine learning model that can accurately predict the eligibility of customers for home loans based on their demographic and financial details provided in the online application form. The model should classify customers into segments based on their likelihood of loan approval, enabling the company to target specific customer groups effectively.

Hypothesis

Using machine learning algorithms on historical data, customers' eligibility for a loan can be accurately predicted. The Algorithm will use the demographics of the customers, such as gender, marital status, education, number of dependents, and others, such as income, loan amount, and credit history, to analyse and classify customers into segments based on their likelihood of loan approval. These segments can help the company target specific customers by tailoring their loan offerings and marketing strategies to maximise approval rates and reduce risks. These strategies can lead to customer satisfaction and business growth.

Collection and Preparation

The first step was to collect data. The historical data contains customer details such as gender, marital status, education, number of dependents, income, loan amount, credit history, etc. And it encompasses various customers across different demographics and loan scenarios. After this, the data is cleaned, which involves handling missing values, encoding categorical variables, scaling numerical features, and feature engineering, which is performed to extract additional insights.

Model Selection and Training

This project is a classification task. Machine learning algorithms such as logistic regression, decision trees, random forests, and gradient-boosting machines are considered. The dataset is split into training and testing sets to evaluate the models' performance.
