Sales Analysis

Project Overview

The goal was to gain insights into sales patterns and predict the Sales amount using multiple regression models.

Objectives

This project is aimed at Analysis for the BlackFriday Sales of a company in 3 cities and prediction of the Sales amount. The dataset, sourced from Kaggle, contains various features related to customer purchases and other sales-related factors. the company intends to gain insights into customer purchasing patterns and factors influencing purchase amounts. This understanding will help in strategic decision-making for inventory management, marketing strategies, and enhancing customer satisfaction..

Goals

  • Analyse Purchase Patterns: Determine the most popular products and categories and understand the factors influencing purchase amounts
  • Predict Purchase Amounts: Develop a predictive model to forecast purchase amounts based on customer demographics, product categories, and other relevant factors.
  • Enhance Customer Targeting: Recommend targeted marketing strategies to improve customer retention and sales.
  • Identify Key Customer Segments: Segment customers based on purchasing behaviour and purchase amounts

Methodology

  • Data source: Kaggle.com
  • Data Cleaning and Preprocessing: Handle missing values, outliers, and data inconsistencies to prepare a clean dataset for analysis.s
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Performed EDA to identify trends, patterns, and relationships in the data.
  • Predictive Modelling: Developed machine learning models to predict purchase amounts based on historical data.
  • Segmentation Analysis: Used clustering techniques to segment customers based on purchasing behaviour.
